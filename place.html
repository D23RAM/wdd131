Suriname

About Suriname

Suriname is the smallest country in South America and is located on the northeastern coast of the continent. The country is known for its incredible cultural diversity, rainforests, food, and historic dutch colonial architecture.

The capital city, Paramaribo, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site because of its unique wooden colonial buildings. Suriname has a rich mixture of cultures, including Indigenous, Indian, African, Javanese, Chinese, and European influences.
